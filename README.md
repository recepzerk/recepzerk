# Recep Zerk

**Digital Literacy Advocate & Independent Researcher**

I research the intersection of algorithmic systems, online identity, and human agency. My work examines how digital environments shape behavior, self-perception, and wellbeing — and what individuals, educators, and platform designers can do about it.

---

## Research

**The Digital Wellbeing Index**
A five-dimension framework for measuring human health in the digital age. Introduces Screen Quality, Algorithmic Dependency, Identity Coherence, Social Comparison Load, and Digital Boundaries as measurable indicators of digital wellbeing.
[View on Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/20149107)

**The Algorithmic Cage**
An investigation into how recommendation systems constrain human agency, reduce discovery variance, and reshape cultural output.
[View on SlideShare](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/the-algorithmic-cage-report-2026-the-state-of-human-agency-in-a-predictive-world/286501382)

**Digital Identity Drift**
An academic report examining how sustained online self-presentation gradually reshapes real-life identity, psychological well-being, and social behavior.
[View on SlideShare](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/digital-identity-drift-how-online-profiles-reshape-real-life-identity-and-well-being-by-recep-zerk/287276029)

**AI Visibility Report**
How brands gain visibility and recommendations in the AI-driven discovery era.
[View on SlideShare](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/how-brands-gain-visibility-and-recommendations-in-the-ai-driven-discovery-era-by-2026/286665061)
